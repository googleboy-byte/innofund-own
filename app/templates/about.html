About InnoFund

Revolutionizing Research Funding Through Web3

What is InnoFund?

InnoFund is a pioneering Web3-based DeFi application designed to transform how research papers are funded. Our platform creates a bridge between researchers and supporters, enabling decentralized funding for groundbreaking research projects.

How It Works

Submit Project

Researchers create and submit their research projects to the platform

Community Review

Projects are reviewed and evaluated by the community through upvotes and feedback

Receive Funding

Approved projects receive funding from supportive donors

Track Progress

Researchers can track their project's funding progress and community engagement

Quality Control

Community can report suspicious projects for admin review to maintain platform integrity

Admin Review

Dedicated admin dashboard to review reported projects and ensure platform safety
